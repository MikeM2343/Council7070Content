## Meetings

Meetings are normally held the **third Wednesday** of the month at **7:30 p.m.** at the St. Peter Columbus Club hall located at **2947 66 St NW, Edmonton, AB T6K 4C1**

However, due to COVID restrictions all meetings will be held virtually until further notice.
