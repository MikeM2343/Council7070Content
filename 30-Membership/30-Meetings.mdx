Planning meetings are held on the **second Wednesday** of the month at **7:30 p.m.** at the St. Peter Columbus Club hall located at **2947 66 St NW, Edmonton, AB T6K 4C1**.  This used to be an Executive meeting, but now it is open to all members of the Council.

The general meeting is held on the **third Wednesday** of the month, also at **7:30 p.m.**  All are welcome and there are snacks and fraternity following the meeting.
