The annual [Alberta March for Life](https://albertamarchforlife.com/) occurs in May each year starting and ending at the Alberta Legislature building.

It proclaims a peaceful pro-life message to the world, provincial legislators, the media and the public in general.

St. Peter Council typically holds a Novena for Life during the week of the March for Life. Stay tuned for more details
